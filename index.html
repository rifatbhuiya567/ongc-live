mission

We are a leading diversified company carrying out construction, engineering, procurement, development and investment activities internationally. We are committed to providing reliable, amicable, and professional service to our valuable clients, and to being supportive to local businesses and social activities, being friendly to the environment as well as being proactive in the socio-economic environments within which we operate. Our appreciation of our client's interests, evidenced by meeting their requirements and ensuring high-quality work, is the prime directive of our management.

vision

Osos Al Najd is comprised of capable individuals who possess specialized technical know-how, the highest levels of customer service and knowledge of the local market that can only be gained with time and experience.
From the outset, the professionals at ONGC were successful in anticipating the current level of development taking place across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our Commitment

Every aspect of the company is designed to make sure that we might use our global experience and capabilities to meet the unique needs of each client. We believe that the attached information will assist you in evaluating our business, and we look forward to the opportunity to put our administration, construction, maintenance, and engineering abilities to work for you.

Quality Policy

While retaining profitability and competitiveness, ONGC is devoted to providing professional work to our clients that fulfill project standards and requirements for resources, workmanship, tolerances, timelines, and public service. Our company doesn't compromise on quality because quality work is our recognition, and we are known by our quality.

HSE

We are very concerned about the health and safety of the employees. Therefore, we provide a safe environment according to the HSE policy. A health and safety policy outlines your approach to risk management in general. It describes how you, as an operator, will handle workplace health and safety. It should state clearly who is responsible for what, when, and how. Therefore, ONGC has approved HSE certificate which encourages employees to work hard with full determination.

Performance Driven

Our company is very motivated and performance driven. We work very hard to meet the need of our clients. It is one of our good approaches to inspire and reward our employees for being more efficient. Increased staff retention, improved recruiting, and improved performance are just a few of the benefits. Therefore, due to this encouragement the staff is highly performance driven.
ONGC expects to be the preferred option at all times, both locally and internationally, for their well-known excellence, quality, efficiency, and dependability in all types of constructions, utilizing cutting-edge technology and modern conceptions.

Customer-Centric

Our services are centered on our valued customers and their requirements. We create a strategy that is totally in line with the demands and expectations of our clients.
